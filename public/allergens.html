⚠️ Important Notice

Charlie's Wingz is prepared in a kitchen that handles all major allergens including gluten, eggs, dairy, soya, sesame, mustard, celery and sulphites. While we take care to minimise cross-contamination, we cannot guarantee that any product is completely free from traces of these allergens.

If you have a severe or life-threatening allergy, please do not rely solely on this information. Contact us directly before placing your order so we can advise you properly.

Allergen information is correct at the time of publication and based on current recipes. Recipes may change — please check back regularly or contact us if you are unsure.
